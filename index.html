About us

European Ready Mix Company is a premier partner for high-quality ready-mix concrete solutions in Kuwait. With our extensive experience and dedication to excellence, we operate two state-of-the-art RMC plants strategically located in Mutlaa and Sualibia. Together, these plants boast a robust combined capacity of over 20,000 m³ of concrete per month, ensuring we can meet the demands of even the most ambitious projects.

Our Products

Regular Mix

Standard concrete mix suitable for general construction purposes. (K150, K200, K250, K300, K350, K400)

High-Strength Mix

High Strength mix designed for projects requiring extreme strength or durability. (K500 up to K900)

Fiber-Reinforced Mix

Consists of fibers that enhance the concrete's toughness and resistance to cracking using either PP fibers or Steel Fibers.

Lightweight Mix

Ideal for structures where weight reduction is critical, such as upper floors and roofing.

Sustainable Mix

Ideal for structures where weight reduction is critical, such as upper floors and roofing.

Self-Compacting Mix

Eliminates the need for vibration during placement, enhancing workability and finish quality.

Colored Mix

Adds aesthetic appeal with a wide range of colors, suitable for decorative applications.

Specialized Mixes

Secrete, mortars and by partnering with Cemex we can introduce unique concrete materials for heat insulation, concrete without rebar and fast curing concrete.
